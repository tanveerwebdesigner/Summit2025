GROWTH SUMMIT

The EdTech Growth Summit is a premier initiative by NIIT, dedicated to empowering EdTech founders as they navigate the complexities of an evolving economic landscape and the challenges of the post-pandemic era. Now in its fourth edition, this exclusive online Summit provides a transformative platform for entrepreneurs looking to scale their startups into sustainable and high-impact ventures.

At the Summit, participants gain invaluable insights from seasoned investors, industry pioneers, and successful EdTech founders who have built and scaled thriving businesses. Through expert-led discussions, actionable strategy sessions, and real-world case studies, attendees will uncover the secrets to building a market-leading EdTech startup.

Designed for ambitious founders who are passionate about reshaping the future of education, this summit offers a unique opportunity to connect with industry leaders, gain a competitive edge, and accelerate their journey toward becoming India’s next EdTech success story.

Join us and be part of the future of ed-tech.

ABOUT NIIT
NIIT, pioneers in
the learning industry

With 40+ years of transforming lives of millions. We are committed to enable and empower learners with skills that matter.

NIIT is a leading Skills and Talent Development Corporation that is building a manpower pool for global industry requirements. The company, which was set up in 1981 to help the nascent IT industry overcome its human resource challenges, today ranks among the world's leading training companies owing to its vast and comprehensive array of talent development programs. With a footprint in over 30 countries, NIIT offers training and development solutions to Individuals, Enterprises and Institutions.

With a strong focus on assuming leadership role in the Digital Learning World and to further accelerate NIIT's growth and profitability, the company has charted a focused business strategy to address the Digital Transformation needs for individuals and corporate customers by offering training and consulting to help them re-invent themselves & their organisations.

NIIT has two main lines of business across the globe – Corporate Learning Group and Skills & Careers Business.
